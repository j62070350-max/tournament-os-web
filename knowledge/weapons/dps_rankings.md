# Weapon DPS Rankings

All DPS values from the community master database. Assumes 100% accuracy within optimal range.

**Sustained DPS** = Damage per magazine ÷ (fire delay + empty time + reload). Best for long fights.
**Mag Dump DPS** = Damage per magazine ÷ (fire delay + empty time). Best for burst windows.
